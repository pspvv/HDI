What is HDI?

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a composite measure of life expectancy, education, and per capita income — ranking countries into Very High, High, Medium, and Low development tiers.

Our ML model helps policymakers, researchers, and analysts explore development outcomes instantly with interactive charts, and confidence scores.
